Hello and thank you for downloading the pack.

The pack contains 5 low poly aircrafts models specially designed for 
low end devices like mobile games, AR and VR projects for better experience.
All the models are tested in Unity3D (BRP) 2022.3.27f1. 

All Models are UV mapped and sharing the atlas texture and it is in the package.
